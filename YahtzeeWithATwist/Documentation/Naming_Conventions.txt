


Front-end controls
===========================
All controls should have a prefix based on control type
Use the initials of the control type
Examples:
- TextBox			tb_  ...	
- TextBlock			tbl_ ...
- Image				img_ ...
- GridView			gv_  ...




Classes and Strucutres and Other Types:
- Title Cased and Camel Cased
  "ExampleOfClassName"




Data Elements:
- Typically camel cased
- Fields associated with properties are prefixed with '_'
  Private field "_userId" is associated with property "userId"
- Constants are ALL CAPS
